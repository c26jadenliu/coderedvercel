Leadership Team

Meet the people guiding the mission.

Founder and CEO

Jaden

An influential video editor from the Fortnite and VALORANT scenes, Jaden is the founder and CEO of Project Code Red. He is from Los Angeles, CA and now attends the United States Air Force Academy. He created Project Code Red in an effort to prove that the gaming community does not have to conform to its toxic stigmas, and instead can use its power for positivity.

Chief Financial Officer

Alex

From Arcadia, California, Alex is the CFO of Project Code Red. He plays VALORANT, Minecraft, Left 4 Dead 2, Rocket League, TF2, and more. He wants to spread positivity and loves when people enjoy video games as an outlet for fun rather than rage. Joining Code Red helped him express and share these values on a wider scale to a similar, ever-growing community.

Senior Executive Assistant

Yash

Yash is the Senior Executive Assistant of Project Code Red from Irvine, CA. He wants to help make a difference in his local areas while utilizing the power of the gaming community. He is the co-founder of SiNET Tutoring and has organized over 2000 hours of faculty-recognized tutoring sessions to help his local schools and education system.

Chief Operating Officer

Daniel

Daniel is the COO of Project Code Red from Los Angeles, CA. He directs the operations of the team with the goal of creating a difference in the community. He is committed to spreading positivity and leadership values. Creating positive communities has always been his passion, and joining the team has brought him one step closer to that goal.

Programs Outreach Director

Kyle

As Programs Outreach Director, Kyle is dedicated to looking out for people in need and supporting those who are struggling the most. He is an avid Fortnite, VALORANT, OSU, and Genshin player, who also enjoys photography in the Orange County area.

Merchandise Sales Manager

Eugene

Eugene is the Code Red Merchandise Sales Manager from Arcadia, CA who is committed to working towards each client's needs. He enjoys working in Blender and playing video games in his free time.

DragonSaver

DragonSaver is a retired semi-pro competitive Fortnite player. After ending his competitive run, he has dedicated his life to spiritual development and loving everyone no matter who they are or where they come from. He is taking a break from gaming but still is more than happy to help others within the community and the world.
