Jaden

An influential video editor from the Fortnite and VALORANT scenes, Jaden is the founder and CEO of Project Code Red. He is from Los Angeles, CA and now attends the United States Air Force Academy. He created Project Code Red in an effort to prove that the gaming community does not have to conform to its toxic stigmas, and instead can use its power for positivity.
