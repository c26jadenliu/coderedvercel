Leadership Team

The management roster, redesigned around clarity.

Founder and CEO

Jaden

An influential video editor from the Fortnite and VALORANT scenes, Jaden founded Project Code Red to prove gaming can be a source of positivity rather than toxicity. He is from Los Angeles, California and attends the United States Air Force Academy.

Chief Financial Officer

Alex

From Arcadia, California, Alex supports the financial side of the organization while championing positivity in gaming. He wants people to enjoy games as an outlet for fun rather than rage.

Senior Executive Assistant

Yash

Yash is from Irvine, California and helps make a difference locally while using gaming as a unifying force. He also co-founded SiNET Tutoring and has organized more than 2,000 hours of tutoring support.

Chief Operating Officer

Daniel

Daniel directs operations for Project Code Red with a focus on leadership, community-building, and meaningful service. Creating positive communities has always been central to his work.

Programs Outreach Director

Kyle

Kyle is dedicated to serving people in need and supporting those who are struggling most. He also brings strong community energy through games, photography, and local outreach in Orange County.

Merchandise Sales Manager

Eugene

Eugene supports merchandise sales and client needs while contributing creative work and operational help. He enjoys Blender and gaming in his free time.
